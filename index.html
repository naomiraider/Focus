Every focus study is:

Anonymous

Your reddit profile is your identity in focus groups. No additional identifying information is required.

Secure

Your answers are confidential and will not be shared. You can be honest.

Incentivized

You'll be compensated and rewarded for being honest and participating in groups that you are invited to. Rewards will vary by group and will be predetermined ahead of time so you can choose.

Valued

focus groups are invite only. The invitation is linked to your reddit profile. If you were chosen for a survey, it's because you said something interesting and they want to know more. What you say matters.

Flexible

focus researchers will explain what the study is before you choose to join. If the timing works, great! You just made some easy money.
